Building a Gaming PC

Half the fun of PC gaming is building your own custom computer! To do that you'll need the following components:

  • Central Processing Unit (CPU)
  • Graphics Processing Unit (GPU)
  • Motherboard
  • Random Access Memory (RAM) modules
  • Storage Drive(s)
    • Solid-state drive (SSD)
    • Hard disk drive (HDD)
  • Power Supply Unit (PSU)
  • Chassis

Use a handy site like PC Part Picker to choose compatible parts and plan your build. Parts are sold by popular retailers like Newegg, Amazon, Best Buy, and Micro Center. YouTube is full of helpful tutorials and guides for assembly. And for advice on how NOT to build a PC, check out this notorious video from The Verge.
